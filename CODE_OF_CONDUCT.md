# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want AgentMem to be a project where anyone can ask a question, report a bug, or send
a patch without being talked down to. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Standards

Things that keep this a good place to work:

- Assume good faith and be patient with questions, everyone was new once.
- Critique code and ideas, not people.
- Accept that "no" is a complete answer, and that maintainers' time is finite.

Things that aren't welcome:

- Harassment, personal attacks, or demeaning comments, public or private.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussions.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces, issues, pull requests, discussions, and when
representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **conduct@agentmem.xyz**. Reports are handled
confidentially. Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban
participants whose behavior they judge inappropriate, and will try to explain their
reasoning when they do.
